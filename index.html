Welcome to Open-Lang

Open-Lang is an idea. A language entirely designed by popular vote. Every aspect, including the name, is up for vote. A true stab at a crowd-sourced language!

Status and updates

Right now, we've got nothing! As we flesh out the language, finalize grammars, decide design paradigms, I will update this page and the repo to reflect the current status of our language.

Contribution

If you'd like to contribute, please fork the repository and send me an email at kitfreddura@gmail.com
